The Complete PhD Research Proposal Writing Service

Each of these elements is essential to present a proposed thesis and requires a lot of attention while framing a PhD proposal. With our PhD research proposal writing service you can be rest assured that your proposal will be written according to the standard set by your university.
Title

A clear, concise and comprehensive title that summarizes the research project and captures the essence of the research.

Introduction

A clear and concise section that presents the background, context and rationale of the research. It includes the problem statement and research questions that the study aims to address.

Need of the study

A section that highlights the gap in existing literature and the importance of conducting the research. It explains how the proposed research project will contribute to the field of study.

Conceptual Framework

A theoretical or conceptual basis of the research that provides a structure for understanding the research problem and objectives. It outlines the key concepts, variables and theoretical constructs that guide the study.

Objectives

Specific, measurable and achievable goals of the research project that are derived from the research question and conceptual framework.

Hypothesis

A statement of predicted relationships or outcomes of the research that will be tested through the study. It is usually developed from the research objectives and conceptual framework.
